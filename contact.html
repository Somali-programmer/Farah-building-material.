Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your delivery options and costs?

We offer free delivery for orders over ETB 5,000 within Jigjiga City. For orders below this amount, delivery costs ETB 200. We also offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 12 PM for an additional ETB 300.

Do you offer bulk discounts for large orders?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders. For orders over ETB 50,000, you can receive up to 15% discount depending on the products. Contact our sales team for a customized quote for your project.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns of unused materials within 14 days of purchase with original packaging. A restocking fee of 10% may apply for certain items. Special order items cannot be returned.

Can I get technical advice for my project?

Absolutely! Our technical team is available to provide advice on material selection, application techniques, and project planning. You can schedule a free consultation by calling our technical department.
