Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What is the minimum order for bulk pricing?

Our bulk pricing applies to orders of ETB 50,000 or more. For orders between ETB 25,000 and ETB 50,000, we offer a 5% discount. Contact our sales team for specific pricing on large projects.

Do you offer emergency delivery services?

Yes, we offer emergency delivery for an additional fee. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 12 PM. After-hours and weekend deliveries are also possible with advance notice.

Can you source materials not listed on your website?

Absolutely! We have relationships with suppliers worldwide and can source specialized materials upon request. Please contact us with your specific requirements, and we'll provide availability and pricing.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver throughout Jigjiga City and the surrounding Somali Regional State areas. Delivery to more remote locations may incur additional charges. Contact us for specific delivery information to your project site.

Do you provide material estimates for projects?

Yes, our technical team can provide detailed material estimates based on your project plans. This service is complimentary for projects with an estimated material cost of ETB 100,000 or more.
